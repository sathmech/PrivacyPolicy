Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Email Extractor extension. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information collected through our extension.

2. Information We Collect

Our extension extracts email addresses from web pages but does not store, share, or transmit this data to any external server.

3. How We Use the Information

Any email addresses collected by the extension are processed locally on your device and are not sent to any third party.

4. Permissions

The extension requires permissions to access web page content to extract email addresses, but it does not track browsing history or store any data beyond user interactions.

5. Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services.

6. Security

Your extracted email data remains on your device. We do not collect or transmit any sensitive data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

8. Contact Us
