Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 14, 2025

1. Introduction

Cache Killer ("the extension") is designed to automatically clear the browser cache for better performance. We respect your privacy and do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

2. Information We Collect

This extension does not collect or transmit any personal data. However, it may use Chrome's local storage to save user preferences (such as enabling or disabling cache clearing).

3. Permissions Used

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • browsingData: To clear the browser cache when necessary.
  • storage: To store extension settings locally on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services.

5. Security

Your data is not collected or transmitted. The extension operates entirely on your local device.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
