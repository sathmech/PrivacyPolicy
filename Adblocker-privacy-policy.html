Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 14, 2025

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how our Ad Blocker Chrome extension operates and handles your data.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The extension operates locally on your browser and does not transmit any browsing data to external servers.

3. Permissions

The extension requires certain permissions to function correctly:

  • Access to web pages: This is needed to detect and remove ads.
  • Storage: Used only to remember per-tab ad-blocking settings.

4. Third-Party Services

This extension does not use third-party analytics, tracking, or external APIs.

5. Security

We take security seriously. The extension does not inject harmful scripts or track users' activities.
